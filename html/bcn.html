BCN RAKE HANDLING

Hind Terminals (HTPL) has been facilitating Agricultural and Steel product handling through BCN Rakes at Rail Link Facility located in the Nhava Sheva area through its strategic alliance. This Rail linked facility is equipped with all the infrastructures required for EXIM business and is connected by Rail as well as by Road.

HTPL offers services such as cargo unloading, storage and stuffing into EXIM containers for Export. Dedicated availability of labours and equipment allows 24x7 operations.
